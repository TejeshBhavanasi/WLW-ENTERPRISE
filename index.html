About US

At W.L.W. Enterprise, we don’t just build businesses; we develop partnerships that help organisations thrive. Founded with a mission to empower companies through strategic marketing, promotion, and engagement, we pride ourselves on our ability to tailor solutions that meet the unique needs of each business we support. Our focus is on creating sustainable growth through cutting-edge solutions that drive results.

  • Our Vision

    "To become a leading force in connecting businesses and organisations globally, fostering innovation and growth through seamless integration and cutting-edge solutions."

  • Our Mission

    "To empower businesses by providing tailored solutions that enhance collaboration, efficiency, and scalability. We strive to create value by integrating the latest technologies and best practices to drive sustainable success for our clients."

Portfolio

Portfolio of Connected Businesses

Youngstown Event Center

Located at 12 East Dewey Avenue, Youngstown, Ohio, the Youngstown Event Center is your go-to destination for unforgettable events. Whether you’re planning a wedding, corporate event, or a private party, we offer a versatile space with comprehensive services like catering, decoration, and event management to make your occasion perfect.

Host your next event with us!

Crawling to Destiny

At Crawling to Destiny (C2D), we believe every child has the potential to grow, learn, and succeed. Our nurturing environment, experienced educators, and engaging programs ensure that each child receives the personalized attention they need to thrive.

Join a community where children grow and succeed!

New Vision Behavioral Health Services

New Vision Behavioral Health Services is dedicated to improving the well-being of individuals in our community. With a focus on personalized care, we offer mental health services designed to help people rebuild their lives and achieve long-term health.

Support your mental health with our expert care.

Hitchcock Woods Learning Center

Hitchcock Woods Learning Center offers premier early childhood education, creating a foundation for lifelong learning. Our programs encourage children to explore, discover, and thrive in a safe, supportive environment.

Help your child reach their full potential.
